About Us

Welcome to Punjab Restaurant, where tradition meets taste. We take pride in serving authentic Indian cuisine with rich flavors and fresh ingredients. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, offering a blend of traditional spices and modern culinary expertise.

Established in the heart of Westlands, our restaurant is a warm and inviting space perfect for families, friends, and food lovers alike. Whether you're here for a quick lunch, a cozy dinner, or a festive celebration, we promise an unforgettable dining experience.

We are dedicated to providing the highest quality food and service, ensuring every meal is a delight. Come and taste the true essence of India at Punjab Restaurant!
